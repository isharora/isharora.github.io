About Me
Hello! I am Ishita Arora, a citizen of the Bay Area and a recent college graduate. At UC Berkeley, I majored in mathematics and economics, and also took a variety of classes to satisfy my interest in Russian literature, web design, entrepreneurship, and social justice. One of my favorite indulgences is enjoying a hot cup of tea and reading a juicy mystery. I also enjoy traveling and will be exploring Southeast Asia in the summer of 2015. After returning from my adventures, I will be starting my professional career at Dropbox, as a part of the rotation program. I love meeting new people, so don't be a stranger -- let's meet up, share a pot of tea, and share our stories.
